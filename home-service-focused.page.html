Real Projects, Real Results

Field Data Collection
Complete Reserve Management System Nature Seychelles • Cousin Island Special Reserve

The Challenge: Field staff needed to track 11 different conservation activities—from turtle patrols to seabird monitoring—all on paper forms that took hours to transcribe.

The Solution: Custom field app (ARM) covering all conservation modules, running on rugged tablets that work in rain and sun. Data syncs automatically and generates reports.

The Result: Staff trained in 4 weeks, now using tablets daily. Data collection is faster, more accurate, and reports are automatic.
Citizen Science
Community Wetland Monitoring Nature Seychelles • LEAP Project

The Challenge: Local communities wanted to help monitor mangrove health but needed a tool available in Seychellois Creole that non-experts could use.

The Solution: Citizen science app (WET) with simple photo-based data collection, available in Creole, English, and French.

The Result: Community members can now contribute valuable monitoring data without technical training.
Wildlife Tracking
Long-Range Tortoise Tracking Nature Seychelles • Cousin Island

The Challenge: Researchers needed to track Aldabra giant tortoises across a large reserve with no cellular coverage and batteries that last years.

The Solution: LoRaWAN GPS tracking system with geofencing alerts and 5-year battery life, designed for remote deployment.

The Result: Real-time tracking data showing tortoise movement patterns and habitat use without constant battery changes.
